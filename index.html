JOBFAIR 2018

Sree Chithra Thirunal College of Engineering, Trivandrum as part of its national techno cultural fest CULT A WAY brings to you the largest off-campus recruitment drive in Kerala. We believe that our innovative venture is capable of transforming the lives of many competent young individuals.The event which is successfully entering its 6th edition boasts of participants from diverse demographic locations across Kerala. We presume that the esteemed presence of your company will give the right impetus to unleash the true potential of each participant and to propel their personal and professional life to its zenith. Thereby we proudly invite you to JOB FAIR 2018 held at our prestigious college, to guide and direct aspiring individuals towards a career of infinite possibilities.

What makes us different

Fully functional servers for online pre-tests

Fully equipped halls and volunteers

Highly enthusiastic team exclusively for each company

A common aptitude test for a cluster of companies

Facility for group discussion or group activity

Database of over 1500 potential candidates

OUR LEGACY

We have successfully hosted 5 editions of JOB FAIR which saw overwhelming response from all over Kerala. Our previous editions saw a consistent footfall of two thousand or more candidates. We hold the testimony of a number of top recruiters and the happy faces of thousands of candidates who walked out of here with a call letter. Hosted by one of the most efficient Placement Units in Kerala, CGPU SCTCE we promise you an off-campus recruitment like never before.

WHAT CAN YOU DO?

It is your one time opportunity to associate with an event which offers you great exposure to potential candidates and at the same time improve your crowd appeal, reputation and global stature. The event will help you attract more young blood.We positively believe that you’ll accept this invitation and become part of our initiative to unlock wide range of possibilities to young individuals in these times of turmoil.
