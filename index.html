#UNLOCK YOUR FUTURE

Sree Chithra Thirunal College of Engineering, Trivandrum as part of its national techno cultural fest ‘CULT A WAY’ brings to you the largest off-campus recruitment drive in Kerala. We believe that our innovative venture is capable of transforming the lives of many competent young individuals.The event which is successfully entering its 4th edition boasts of participants from diverse demographic locations across Kerala. We presume that the esteemed presence of your company will give the right impetus to unleash the true potential of each participant and to propel their personal and professional life to its zenith. Thereby we proudly invite you to ‘JOB FAIR 2017’ held at our prestigious college from 29th to 30th of July 2017, to guide and direct aspiring individuals towards a career of infinite possibilities.

OUR LEGACY

We have successfully hosted four editions of JOB FAIR which saw overwhelming response from all over Kerala. Our previous editions saw a consistent footfall of two thousand or more candidates. We hold the testimony of a number of top recruiters and the happy faces of thousands of candidates who walked out of here with a call letter. Hosted by one of the most efficient Placement Units in Kerala, CGPU SCTCE we promise you an off-campus recruitment like never before.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT?

Unlike conventional job fairs which end up as CV collection events, we choose to differ. JOBFAIR 2017 offers you the ultimate platform to zero in on the right employee for your organisation. We provide you with a platform and tools to properly assess the candidates.

  • Fully functional servers for online pre-tests.
  • Fully equipped halls and volunteers for offline tests.
  • A group of highly enthusiastic volunteer team exclusively for each company.
  • A common aptitude test for a cluster of companies. (If required)
  • Arrangements for proper conduction of interviews.
  • Database of over 1500 potential candidates. (Only on request)

WHAT CAN YOU DO?

It is your one time opportunity to associate with an event which offers you great exposure to potential candidates and at the same time improve your crowd appeal, reputation and global stature. The event will help you attract more young blood.We positively believe that you’ll accept this invitation and become part of our initiative to unlock wide range of possibilities to young individuals in these times of turmoil.
